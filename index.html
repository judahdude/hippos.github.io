how long can a hippo hold its breath?

Suprisingly hippos can only hold their breath for 5 minutes

how much does a hippo weigh?

males weigh from 3300 to 4000 pounds!
females only weigh 2900 to 3300.
warning! hippos are very self conscious about their weight, so dont be asking "hey how fat are you?"

fighting

hippos are very agressive and dangerous, they have large teeth and tusks that are used for fighting off threats...or starting them. The young hippos sadly sometimes get caught in the middle of two fighting males and die.

what do hippos eat?

hippos mostly eat grass and tons of it too! they eat about 80 to 100 pounds of food a day! but they have been seen eating meat so dont get too close
